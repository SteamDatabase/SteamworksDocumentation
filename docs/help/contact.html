Contacting Valve / Steam

Many topics and issues are well documented here in Steamworks Documentation or can be found by searching the Steamworks Developer Discussion Boards.

Steamworks Support Tickets

If you can't find what you are looking for in the documentation, please use this link below to contact Steamworks developer support for personal assistance from one of our representatives.

Open a Support Ticket: https://help.steampowered.com/en/wizard/HelpWithPublishing

Steamworks Developer Discussions

If you are looking for advice or have a question about how things are typically handled for particular situations, the best place to start is with the Steamworks Developer Discussions. Here you'll find other game developers that can answer your question or provide advice based on their own feedback. Plus, many members of the Steam Team are active, answering questions as needed as well.
