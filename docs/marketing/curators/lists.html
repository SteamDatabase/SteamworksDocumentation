Curator Lists For Steam Events

Steam Curators can associate a list of reviewed games to a Steam Sale (for example: Steam Fighting Game Sale) or an Event (for example: Steam Game Festival) that will automatically be displayed to the curator's followers. This allows curators to help guide their fans on exploring the sale's featured games.

The sale or event operator may optionally choose to feature one or more curators to help provide recommendation guidance for Steam customers who may not be logged in or who do not follow any relevant curators.

In brief

To participate with a list in this way, you'll need to follow these steps:
  1. Write a brief review for each game in the festival, sale, or event that you want to help your followers discover
  2. Create a list that includes all those reviewed games
  3. Associate that list with the intended event

Requirements

  • You'll need a Steam Curator page in good standing
  • Your account will need 'manage page admin settings and review games' permission within your Steam Curator group.

Setting up a list

  1. On your curator group's store page (for example: example curator), start by choosing the 'Admin Tab' found at the top and then choosing 'Create New List' which is found on the left side.



  2. Enter a title and a description of the list. These will be shown prominently when the list is display on a Steam Sale page. This is the key information the users has to decide whether they want to leave the sale to explore your recommendations.

  3. Next add one or more games to the list that are featured on the sales page. Please remember that it will only allow adding games to the list that have previously been reviewed by your curator.

    If you just created a review, it often takes up to 5 minutes for the list to refresh with the updated list of reviews. The review text provided during the review will be used and displayed on the list.
    Note: Games that are not featured in the sales will not be presented to the Steam customer





  4. Choose from the drop down the Steam Event to associate with.



    Its important to remember that only once an event has started and the event is visible to Steam customers then it will appear in this list. The event will only present in the list for up to one month after the event has started

    This action associates this list with the Steam sale/event. This also will act as a filter, once associated, only games featured in the Steam sale/event will appear on the user visible page

  5. Optional: You may enter a URL for a Youtube video to display at the top of your list page. This should be video that covers the games in your list. For instance, it might be play through or a mixed teasers of the various games. The video will be included at the top of the list and will be immediately playable for Steam customer that is visiting your list.

    URL format:
    https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=<video id>

  6. If the list is associated with sale, then it will appear to all of your followers on the sale page, regardless on whether the list is marked unlisted or public. The 'Unlisted' setting only removes the list from the curator list page or appearing in the appropriate carousel on your curator home page. The 'unlisted' setting has no effect on inclusion on sales page for your followers or being featured on them by the sales operator

    If you do not want a list to appear on the sale page, then do not associate it with the sale as described in the earlier step, until you are ready.
