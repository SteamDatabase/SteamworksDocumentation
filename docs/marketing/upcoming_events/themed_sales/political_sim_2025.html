Steam Political Sim Fest 2025



This Fest is for games where you get to guide a nation - to victory, to riches, or perhaps to ruin. Political Sim Fest is for games about politicking - the kind where you, the leader of a nation, navigate the tricky world of international politics while managing your nation at home.

This event runs September 8 at 10:00 AM PDT (-07:00) to September 15 at 10:00 AM PDT (-07:00) and will include eligible titles with sections for discounted titles, unreleased titles in Coming Soon mode, games with demos, and more.

Important Dates

  • September 4, 2025 - The trailer announcing the event launches on this day. At this point, you're welcome to share your participation in the event. Assets for this purpose will be available in the Asset Kit below.
  • September 8 at 10:00 AM PDT (-07:00) - The event starts. This is the deadline for entering a discount, and opting out if you no longer want to participate.

Eligibility

Games eligible for this Fest will include some combination of:
  • Factions (Party vs. Party, Military vs. Business, etc.)
  • Multiple options for ruling (democratic, autocratic, oligarchic, etc.)
  • Interest groups vying for attention/power
  • Some element of international diplomacy (eg. managing allies / enemies)

However, games that are primarily about warfare and games that were in 4X Fest are not eligible. If your title is both a 4X title and a political sim, please pick the ones that your game aligns more closely against.

Aside from that, the following will render a game ineligible:
  • Significant portions of gameplay focusing on combat, city building, or other non-politicking affairs
  • Games where you play a god where you shape society indirectly / directly
  • Games where you play a Judge, a lowly government worker, or some other cog in the machine that is not the leader - games where you can be elected to a powerful position are eligible
  • Games featuring politicians, but are not political sims, are not eligible
  • Games about the impact of government (eg. Orwellian games, surveillance titles, parody titles, political commentary) are not eligible for this event

Check your list of eligible games here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/political_sim_2025

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. I haven't received an email invitation and have a game I believe fits the eligibility criteria described above. How can I confirm whether my game has been invited and request an invitation if it has not been invited?
A. If you haven't received an email invitation and believe you have a game that fits the eligibility criteria described above, please check:

  • Your list of eligible games here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/political_sim_2025
  • The registration page for your game by appending your appID here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/political_sim_2025/
  • Your base game's App Admin page (in the Marketing Tools & Data section) for the opt-in by appending your appID here: https://partner.steamgames.com/apps/landing/

If your game is not found via the paths above and you believe it meets the eligibility criteria, please use the appeals process available at the bottom of the registration page.

If your game was found via the paths above but you didn't receive an email, please check your Steamworks communication preferences (instructions available in the Steam Themed Sale Events parent documentation page).

Q. Do I need to register my game before entering a discount?
A. Yes, you need to do the following things in order:
  1. Follow the link in the invitation email, or click this link to see your eligible games and register each: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/political_sim_2025
  2. Once a game is registered, you can then enter discounts on the Discount Management page: https://partner.steamgames.com/promotion/discounts/dashboard/?12402

Q. Will there be livestreaming as part of this event?
A. Livestreaming will not be featured on the sale page, but you're welcome to livestream on your own game's store page as you wish.

Q. I've changed my plans. How do I cancel my registration?
A. Just return to the registration page for your game by inserting your appID at the end of this URL https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/political_sim_2025/<appid> and deselect the checkbox at the very bottom of the page to remove your game from the event.

Q. Can I promote our participation in the event on our own channels before the trailer goes live?
A. Technically yes; you can publish once the Asset Kit is available for download, but we recommend waiting until the event trailer is live (again, roughly 4 days prior to the event) as a more effective communications beat with your audience.
