Steam Detective Fest 2026



Get your magnifying glass and notebook handy, because Steam Detective Fest is all about games where you play a detective - professional, amateur, or even unwilling - solving case(s) of all kinds.

Eligibility

Eligible games will feature one or more mysteries that serve as the primary focus or gameplay loop of the game.

These games are likely to include:
  • Clues to be 'found' or deduced via mini-game or puzzle
  • Some kind of deduction process - it's not enough to solve each mini-game or only gather the clues, the player must 'put it together' in the end somehow.
  • A notebook where the player can review the clues they've gained so far
  • Multiple ways of acquiring clues - interviewing suspects/witnesses, taking a closer look at specific parts of the crime scene, etc.

Eligible games will be easily identifiable as a detective game first and foremost - games that feature a big mystery but the player is not actively solving for it are not eligible.

Types of genres that might disqualify a game:
  • Police games - detectives on the police force are fine, but if the game focuses on things like patrolling, making arrests, assigning tickets, and shootouts, it may not be a fit
  • Social deduction games - if there is no 'big mystery' and it's a round-based title, then that's not eligible
  • Hidden Object games - there's a separate Hidden Object Fest for these titles
  • Strong gameplay elements from a different genre - like shooting, platforming, or racing

As an example, a shooting game where the main character is being targeted by a mysterious person/group would not be eligible if the game mostly focuses on shooting rather than mystery-solving.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. I haven't received an email invitation and have a game I believe fits the eligibility criteria described above. How can I confirm whether my game has been invited and request an invitation if it has not been invited?
A. If you haven't received an email invitation and believe you have a game that fits the eligibility criteria described above, please check:

  • Your list of eligible games here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/detective_2026
  • The registration page for your game by appending your appID here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/detective_2026/
  • Your base game's App Admin page (in the Marketing Tools & Data section) for the opt-in by appending your appID here: https://partner.steamgames.com/apps/landing/

If your game is not found via the paths above and you believe it meets the eligibility criteria, please use the appeals process available at the bottom of the registration page.

If your game was found via the paths above but you didn't receive an email, please check your Steamworks communication preferences (instructions available in the Steam Themed Sale Events parent documentation page).

Q. Do I need to register my game before entering a discount?
A. Yes, you need to do the following things in order:
  1. Follow the link in the invitation email, or click this link to see your eligible games and register each: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/detective_2026
  2. Once a game is registered, you can then enter discounts on the Discount Management page: https://partner.steamgames.com/promotion/discounts/dashboard/?14041

Q. Will there be livestreaming as part of this event?
A. Livestreaming will not be featured on the sale page, but you're welcome to livestream on your own game's store page as you wish.

Q. I've changed my plans. How do I cancel my registration?
A. Just return to the registration page for your game by inserting your appID at the end of this URL https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/detective_2026/<appid> and deselect the checkbox at the very bottom of the page to remove your game from the event.

Q. Can I promote our participation in the event on our own channels before the trailer goes live?
A. Technically yes; you can publish once the Asset Kit is available for download, but we recommend waiting until the event trailer is live (again, roughly 4 days prior to the event) as a more effective communications beat with your audience.
