Steam Automation Fest 2025



This Fest is for the games that have you thinking "hm, this belt is in the way of this other belt, so I just need to move a few things around and then I'll shorten the time to build that thing..." and then it's 2am and you haven't finished building.

Automation Fest is for games that feature heavy automation as a primary gameplay loop. Does your game feature multiple machines with an input and an output? Do you have teleporters, conveyor/transport belts, tubes, robotic arms, lifts, or other objects that help goods move from one side of your factory to another? The name of the game for these games is efficiency; eligible titles will be focused around the idea of getting from input A to input B in the most efficient manner. The idea of a production chain should be one that your game is familiar with.

This event is not for games where you're optimizing things like crop yields for your population, or trade routes so your kingdom can stay alive, or min-maxing gold bar creation in order to fund your offers. It's fine for your game to have an end goal, but the primary gameplay loop should be about optimizing for optimizations sake; no lives should be at stake!

This event runs July 14 at 10:00 AM PDT (-07:00) to July 21 at 10:00 AM PDT (-07:00) and will include eligible titles with sections for discounted titles, unreleased titles in Coming Soon mode, games with demos, and more.

  • July 10, 2025 - The trailer announcing the event launches on this day. At this point, you're welcome to share your participation in the event. Assets for this purpose will be available in the Asset Kit below.
  • July 14 at 10:00 AM PDT (-07:00) - The event starts. This is the deadline for entering a discount, and opting out if you no longer want to participate.

Eligibility

Games where the automation comes from minions that mindlessly move things around are not eligible, nor are games where your character gets better at creating objects. Puzzles that are solved by creating efficient pathways to take an input and generate the desired output, or programming puzzles where there are multiple solutions (and ways to improve your solution) are allowed.

If you have to care about something other than if you have enough materials to create more things, such as hunger, population, or ethics, then your title probably isn't a great fit. Similarly, if your game has heavy shooting, this is probably not the Fest for you!

What it is:

Genre/Tags:
  • Automation
  • Programming puzzle games are okay

What it is not:

Games that are primarily about the following are NOT eligible: 

  • Automation via additional minions
  • Automation via upgrades only; some amount of rearranging and/or pathway placement is necessary
  • Games with heavy shooting elements
  • Games with hunger, population, or ethics
  • Most base builders / colony management titles
  • Most idle games

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. I haven't received an email invitation and have a game I believe fits the eligibility criteria described above. How can I confirm whether my game has been invited and request an invitation if it has not been invited?
A. If you haven't received an email invitation and believe you have a game that fits the eligibility criteria described above, please check:

  • Your list of eligible games here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/automation_2025
  • The registration page for your game by appending your appID here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/automation_2025/
  • Your base game's App Admin page (in the Marketing Tools & Data section) for the opt-in by appending your appID here: https://partner.steamgames.com/apps/landing/

If your game is not found via the paths above and you believe it meets the eligibility criteria, please use the appeals process available at the bottom of the registration page.

If your game was found via the paths above but you didn't receive an email, please check your Steamworks communication preferences (instructions available in the Steam Themed Sale Events parent documentation page).

Q. Do I need to register my game before entering a discount?
A. Yes, you need to do the following things in order:
  1. Follow the link in the invitation email, or click this link to see your eligible games and register each: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/automation_2025
  2. Once a game is registered, you can then enter discounts on the Discount Management page: https://partner.steamgames.com/promotion/discounts/dashboard/?12398

Q. Will there be livestreaming as part of this event?
A. Livestreaming will not be featured on the sale page, but you're welcome to livestream on your own game's store page as you wish.

Q. I've changed my plans. How do I cancel my registration?
A. Just return to the registration page for your game by inserting your appID at the end of this URL https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/automation_2025/<appid> and deselect the checkbox at the very bottom of the page to remove your game from the event.

Q. Can I promote our participation in the event on our own channels before the trailer goes live?
A. Technically yes; you can publish once the Asset Kit is available for download, but we recommend waiting until the event trailer is live (again, roughly 4 days prior to the event) as a more effective communications beat with your audience.
