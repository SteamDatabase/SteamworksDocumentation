Steam Bullet Fest 2026



Projectiles! Everywhere! Either coming for you or coming from you - this Fest is for all types of Bullet games, from the classic Bullet Hells to modern day Survivor-likes.

This event runs June 8 @ 10:00am PST to June 15 @ 10:00am PST and will include eligible titles with sections for discounted titles, unreleased titles in Coming Soon mode, games with demos, and more.

Important Dates

  • June 8 - The trailer announcing the event launches on this day. At this point, you're welcome to share your participation in the event. Assets for this purpose will be available in the Asset Kit below.
  • June 8 @ 10:00am PST - The event starts. This is the deadline for entering a discount, and opting out if you no longer want to participate.

Eligibility

Eligible titles will generally fall into one of two categories:
  • Bullet Hells (you must dodge many bullets coming towards you, typically from a 2D or top-down perspective)
  • Reverse Bullet Hells / Bullet Heavens / Survivor-likes (you are the source of the bullets, and are mowing down hordes of enemies with your projectiles)

Your average shooter game will not be eligible for this Fest. There needs to be a sense of 'overwhelmingness' - either caused by the number of projectiles your character unit is shooting out, or by the number of projectiles you need to dodge. Games where you must aim and reload are generally not going to be good fits.

Most first-person games are not going to be a good fit. Platformers and Metroidvanias are also generally ineligible, unless a high on-screen bullet count is part of gameplay.

Games that include some elements of these titles, but only a small portion of combat, are not eligible.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. I haven't received an email invitation and have a game I believe fits the eligibility criteria described above. How can I confirm whether my game has been invited and request an invitation if it has not been invited?
A. If you haven't received an email invitation and believe you have a game that fits the eligibility criteria described above, please check:

  • Your list of eligible games here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/bullet_2026
  • The registration page for your game by appending your appID here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/bullet_2026/
  • Your base game's App Admin page (in the Marketing Tools & Data section) for the opt-in by appending your appID here: https://partner.steamgames.com/apps/landing/

If your game is not found via the paths above and you believe it meets the eligibility criteria, please use the appeals process available at the bottom of the registration page.

If your game was found via the paths above but you didn't receive an email, please check your Steamworks communication preferences (instructions available in the Steam Themed Sale Events parent documentation page).

Q. Do I need to register my game before entering a discount?
A. Yes, you need to do the following things in order:
  1. Follow the link in the invitation email, or click this link to see your eligible games and register each: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/bullet_2026
  2. Once a game is registered, you can then enter discounts on the Discount Management page: https://partner.steamgames.com/promotion/discounts/dashboard/?15217

Q. Will there be livestreaming as part of this event?
A. Livestreaming will not be featured on the sale page, but you're welcome to livestream on your own game's store page as you wish.

Q. I've changed my plans. How do I cancel my registration?
A. Just return to the registration page for your game by inserting your appID at the end of this URL https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/bullet_2026/<appid> and deselect the checkbox at the very bottom of the page to remove your game from the event.

Q. Can I promote our participation in the event on our own channels before the trailer goes live?
A. Technically yes; you can publish once the Asset Kit is available for download, but we recommend waiting until the event trailer is live (again, roughly 4 days prior to the event) as a more effective communications beat with your audience.
