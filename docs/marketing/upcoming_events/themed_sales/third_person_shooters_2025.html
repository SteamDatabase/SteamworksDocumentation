Steam TPS Fest 2025



What do you get when you combine a gameplay loop about shooting, and a third-person perspective? Yes - you get third-person shooters, and that's what this Fest is all about!



This event runs August 25 @ 10:00am PDT to September 1 @ 10:00am PDT and will include eligible titles with sections for discounted titles, unreleased titles in Coming Soon mode, games with demos, and more.

  • August 25 @ 10:00am PDT - The event starts. This is the deadline for entering a discount, and opting out if you no longer want to participate.

Eligibility

Third-Person Shooter Games Fest are for games that are:
  1. From the third-person perspective
  2. Where the primary gameplay loop is shooting (bullets, water, cupcakes, etc.)

Games with strong elements of other genres (including racing, puzzles, platforming, and stealth) are generally not eligible though it will depend on how much of the game is spent shooting vs. these other activities. Additionally, games played in the third-person that include shooting but has strong gameplay elements of other forms of combat (martial arts, swords, etc.) are not invited to this event.

Horror games where the shooting is to discourage enemies / temporary disable them to allow the player character to run away, rather than completely dispatching them, are also not allowed.

Games that participated in First Person Shooter Fest are not allowed. If your game has both POVs, then the one that customers appear to play your game under is the one that your title is eligible for. For unreleased titles, what is visible via your trailer and screenshots will be used to qualify your title's POV.

Additionally, tower defense titles (where you control a character that is also shooting) are not allowed either.

Check your list of eligible games here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/third_person_shooters_2025

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. I haven't received an email invitation and have a game I believe fits the eligibility criteria described above. How can I confirm whether my game has been invited and request an invitation if it has not been invited?
A. If you haven't received an email invitation and believe you have a game that fits the eligibility criteria described above, please check:

  • Your list of eligible games here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/third_person_shooters_2025
  • The registration page for your game by appending your appID here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/third_person_shooters_2025/
  • Your base game's App Admin page (in the Marketing Tools & Data section) for the opt-in by appending your appID here: https://partner.steamgames.com/apps/landing/

If your game is not found via the paths above and you believe it meets the eligibility criteria, please use the appeals process available at the bottom of the registration page.

If your game was found via the paths above but you didn't receive an email, please check your Steamworks communication preferences (instructions available in the Steam Themed Sale Events parent documentation page).

Q. Do I need to register my game before entering a discount?
A. Yes, you need to do the following things in order:
  1. Follow the link in the invitation email, or click this link to see your eligible games and register each: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/third_person_shooters_2025
  2. Once a game is registered, you can then enter discounts on the Discount Management page: https://partner.steamgames.com/promotion/discounts/dashboard/?12401

Q. Will there be livestreaming as part of this event?
A. Livestreaming will not be featured on the sale page, but you're welcome to livestream on your own game's store page as you wish.

Q. I've changed my plans. How do I cancel my registration?
A. Just return to the registration page for your game by inserting your appID at the end of this URL https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/third_person_shooters_2025/<appid> and deselect the checkbox at the very bottom of the page to remove your game from the event.

Q. Can I promote our participation in the event on our own channels before the trailer goes live?
A. Technically yes; you can publish once the Asset Kit is available for download, but we recommend waiting until the event trailer is live (again, roughly 4 days prior to the event) as a more effective communications beat with your audience.
