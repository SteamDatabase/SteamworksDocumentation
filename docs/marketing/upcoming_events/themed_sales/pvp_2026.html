Steam PvP Fest 2026



In past years we've celebrated Valentine's Day by featuring co-operative games you can play with your loved ones.

Not this year! This year we celebrate PvP games - that is, player versus player games where you fight your friends and loved ones in combat. Whether it's online PvP or local split-screen shenanigans, all games with combat PvP are welcome to join.

This event runs February 9, 2026 @ 10:00am PST to February 16, 2026 @ 10:00am PST and will include eligible titles with sections for discounted titles, unreleased titles in Coming Soon mode, games with demos, and more.


For general information about Steam themed sale events, including details on the selection and invitation process, ways to participate (discounts, demos, and upcoming releases), event visibility and promotion, in-event game visibility, and marketing materials permissions, please visit the Steam Themed Sale Events parent documentation page.

Important Dates

  • February 5, 2026 - The trailer announcing the event launches on this day. At this point, you're welcome to share your participation in the event. Assets for this purpose will be available in the Asset Kit below.
  • February 9, 2026 @ 10:00am PST - The event starts. This is the deadline for entering a discount, and opting out if you no longer want to participate.

Eligibility

Please note that this is called PvP Fest, and not 'online multiplayer Fest'.

This means that your game must feature player versus player combat!

The "Share/Split Screen PvP" or "Online PvP" feature should be selected for your title, and your title should feature combat of some kind.

Social Deduction games that feature the ability to kill or knock out other players are allowed. Games where you beat your opponent by points are not allowed, sorry!

For additional clarity: board/card game type of games are generally not eligible - this Fest is for games where you fight your friends directly, and not through some other medium.

Check your list of eligible games here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/pvp_2026

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. I haven't received an email invitation and have a game I believe fits the eligibility criteria described above. How can I confirm whether my game has been invited and request an invitation if it has not been invited?
A. If you haven't received an email invitation and believe you have a game that fits the eligibility criteria described above, please check:

  • Your list of eligible games here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/pvp_2026
  • The registration page for your game by appending your appID here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/pvp_2026/
  • Your base game's App Admin page (in the Marketing Tools & Data section) for the opt-in by appending your appID here: https://partner.steamgames.com/apps/landing/

If your game is not found via the paths above and you believe it meets the eligibility criteria, please use the appeals process available at the bottom of the registration page.

If your game was found via the paths above but you didn't receive an email, please check your Steamworks communication preferences (instructions available in the Steam Themed Sale Events parent documentation page).

Q. Do I need to register my game before entering a discount?
A. Yes, you need to do the following things in order:
  1. Follow the link in the invitation email, or click this link to see your eligible games and register each: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/pvp_2026
  2. Once a game is registered, you can then enter discounts on the Discount Management page: https://partner.steamgames.com/promotion/discounts/dashboard/?13983

Q. Will there be livestreaming as part of this event?
A. Livestreaming will not be featured on the sale page, but you're welcome to livestream on your own game's store page as you wish.

Q. I've changed my plans. How do I cancel my registration?
A. Just return to the registration page for your game by inserting your appID at the end of this URL https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/pvp_2026/<appid> and deselect the checkbox at the very bottom of the page to remove your game from the event.

Q. Can I promote our participation in the event on our own channels before the trailer goes live?
A. Technically yes; you can publish once the Asset Kit is available for download, but we recommend waiting until the event trailer is live (again, roughly 4 days prior to the event) as a more effective communications beat with your audience.
