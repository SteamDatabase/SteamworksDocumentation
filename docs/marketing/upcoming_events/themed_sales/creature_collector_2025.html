Steam Creature Collector Fest 2025



This Fest is for games about collecting creatures! Fantastical creatures only – walking fruit with eyes, dogs with rockets, flying cat-fish-spider hybrids are all welcome. Extinct animals (ie. dinosaurs) are also welcome.

This event runs May 12 at 10:00 AM PDT (-07:00) to May 19 at 10:00 AM PDT (-07:00) and will include eligible titles with sections for discounted titles, unreleased titles in Coming Soon mode, games with demos, and more.


For general information about Steam themed sale events, including details on the selection and invitation process, ways to participate (discounts, demos, and upcoming releases), event visibility and promotion, in-event game visibility, and marketing materials permissions, please visit the Steam Themed Sale Events parent documentation page.

Important Dates

  • May 12, 2025 - The trailer announcing the event launches on this day. At this point, you're welcome to share your participation in the event. Assets for this purpose will be available in the Asset Kit below.
  • May 12 at 10:00 AM PDT (-07:00) - The event starts. This is the deadline for entering a discount, and opting out if you no longer want to participate.

Eligibility

Eligible games will likely have the following elements:
  • The gameplay loop involves capturing/taming/growing these creatures
  • An album where you can check how many of the creatures you've captured
  • The creatures can be stored somewhere - either with the player, in a box, in a base, or something similar
  • These creatures should do something for the player post-capture; they should be able to fight, be played with, be displayed, etc.

The following features would make a game ineligible:
  • The creatures being collected are real animals (no zoo, aquarium titles)
  • The creatures being collected are non-sentient (plants, viruses, amoebas, etc.)
  • The creatures being collected are randomly generated (creatures must be unique and finite - there needs to be an 'end' to the collection)
  • Tamagotchis, or other games that focus on raising one creature at a time
  • Hunting games where the focus is hunting creatures, rather than collecting them
  • The creature collection is a mini-game or a small part of a different gameplay loop (ie. Farming Sims with creatures are generally not eligible)

Check your list of eligible games here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/creature_collector

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. I haven't received an email invitation and have a game I believe fits the eligibility criteria described above. How can I confirm whether my game has been invited and request an invitation if it has not been invited?
A. If you haven't received an email invitation and believe you have a game that fits the eligibility criteria described above, please check:

  • Your list of eligible games here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/creature_collector
  • The registration page for your game by appending your appID here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/creature_collector/
  • Your base game's App Admin page (in the Marketing Tools & Data section) for the opt-in by appending your appID here: https://partner.steamgames.com/apps/landing/

If your game is not found via the paths above and you believe it meets the eligibility criteria, please use the appeals process available at the bottom of the registration page.

If your game was found via the paths above but you didn't receive an email, please check your Steamworks communication preferences (instructions available in the Steam Themed Sale Events parent documentation page).

Q. Do I need to register my game before entering a discount?
A. Yes, you need to do the following things in order:
  1. Follow the link in the invitation email, or click this link to see your eligible games and register each: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/creature_collector
  2. Once a game is registered, you can then enter discounts on the Discount Management page: https://partner.steamgames.com/promotion/discounts/dashboard/?10603

Q. Will there be livestreaming as part of this event?
A. Livestreaming will not be featured on the sale page, but you're welcome to livestream on your own game's store page as you wish.

Q. I've changed my plans. How do I cancel my registration?
A. Just return to the registration page for your game by inserting your appID at the end of this URL https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/creature_collector/<appid> and deselect the checkbox at the very bottom of the page to remove your game from the event.

Q. Can I promote our participation in the event on our own channels before the trailer goes live?
A. Technically yes; you can publish once the Asset Kit is available for download, but we recommend waiting until the event trailer is live (again, roughly 4 days prior to the event) as a more effective communications beat with your audience.
