Steam Horse Fest 2026



This event is about horses, and other animals from the Equidae family.

As 2026 is the year of the horse, we're here to celebrate all creatures in the horse family! Games eligible for this event will center around a horse - or several horses - or any other animal in the horse family, including zebras, donkeys, and unicorns.

Neigh.

This event runs February 19, 2026 @ 10:00am PST to February 23, 2026 @ 10:00am PST and will include eligible titles with sections for discounted titles, unreleased titles in Coming Soon mode, games with demos, and more.

Important Dates

  • February 16, 2026 - The trailer announcing the event launches on this day. At this point, you're welcome to share your participation in the event. Assets for this purpose will be available in the Asset Kit below.
  • February 19, 2026 @ 10:00am PST - The event starts. This is the deadline for entering a discount, and opting out if you no longer want to participate.

Eligibility

For the following eligibility requirements, any of the other horse-adjacent animals can be substituted in when reviewing your games' eligibility.

These types of games are eligible:
  • Horse Racing titles
  • Horse husbandry / ranch titles
  • Horse protagonists

If your game heavily features a horse, or a significant amount of the game occurs on horseback, you may be eligible if your game also features elements of taking care of said horse.

The following kinds of games are ineligible:
  • Farming Sims where multiple animals can be taken care of, not just horses
  • Games with a wide variety of animals, where horses are one of many options and not the primary focus
  • Games set on horse ranches but do not include horses as part of gameplay


Check your list of eligible games here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/horse_2026

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. I haven't received an email invitation and have a game I believe fits the eligibility criteria described above. How can I confirm whether my game has been invited and request an invitation if it has not been invited?
A. If you haven't received an email invitation and believe you have a game that fits the eligibility criteria described above, please check:

  • Your list of eligible games here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/horse_2026
  • The registration page for your game by appending your appID here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/horse_2026/
  • Your base game's App Admin page (in the Marketing Tools & Data section) for the opt-in by appending your appID here: https://partner.steamgames.com/apps/landing/

If your game is not found via the paths above and you believe it meets the eligibility criteria, please use the appeals process available at the bottom of the registration page.

If your game was found via the paths above but you didn't receive an email, please check your Steamworks communication preferences (instructions available in the Steam Themed Sale Events parent documentation page).

Q. Do I need to register my game before entering a discount?
A. Yes, you need to do the following things in order:
  1. Follow the link in the invitation email, or click this link to see your eligible games and register each: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/horse_2026
  2. Once a game is registered, you can then enter discounts on the Discount Management page: https://partner.steamgames.com/promotion/discounts/dashboard/?13985

Q. Will there be livestreaming as part of this event?
A. Livestreaming will not be featured on the sale page, but you're welcome to livestream on your own game's store page as you wish.

Q. I've changed my plans. How do I cancel my registration?
A. Just return to the registration page for your game by inserting your appID at the end of this URL https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/horse_2026/<appid> and deselect the checkbox at the very bottom of the page to remove your game from the event.

Q. Can I promote our participation in the event on our own channels before the trailer goes live?
A. Technically yes; you can publish once the Asset Kit is available for download, but we recommend waiting until the event trailer is live (again, roughly 4 days prior to the event) as a more effective communications beat with your audience.
