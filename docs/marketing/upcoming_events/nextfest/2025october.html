Steam Next Fest: October 2025


Steam Next Fest: October 2025 Edition is a multi-day celebration where fans can try out demos, chat with developers, watch live streams, and learn about upcoming games on Steam. For developers, Steam Next Fest is an opportunity to get early feedback from players and build an audience for a future launch on Steam.

The October 2025 edition runs October 13 at 10:00 AM PDT (UTC-7) to October 20 at 10:00 AM PDT (UTC-7).


For general information about Steam Next Fest, including details on registration, preparing your demo, running a livestream, trailer and demo gameplay usage, the Press Preview event, game visibility, and frequently asked questions, please visit the Steam Next Fest parent documentation page.

Important Dates

  • August 12, 2025* - Live virtual Q&A session: Learn more and set a reminder here. Takes place on Zoom, 10am - 11am PDT for questions about preparing to participate.
  • August 25 - Registration Deadline (11:59pm PDT). We recommended you have your marketing materials as up-to-date as possible on your base game's store page by this time. This ensures your game will be in the best position to be considered for potential inclusion in official Steam Next Fest marketing materials (main event trailer, genre trailers, etc).
  • August 26 - Steam will notify you if we plan to use your trailer for the official Next Fest trailer.
  • September 2 - If you've been notified of official trailer inclusion consideration, this is the date we'll be pulling your trailer for the official Next Fest trailer. Please ensure your most up to date trailer is available on your store page.
  • September 16 - Recommended date by which you should have your demo build and store assets submitted for review if you wish to release your demo for the press preview.
  • September 29 - Recommended date by which you should have your demo build and store assets submitted for review to release your demo for Steam Next Fest.
  • October 2 - If you've previously opted in to trailer permissions, and have since changed your mind, this is the deadline to opt OUT to ensure your trailer will NOT be included in official marketing materials.
  • October 2 - Press preview starts at 10am PDT.
  • October 13 - Steam Next Fest: October 2025 begins at 10am PDT; set your demo live (or opt out if your plans have changed) before 10am PDT.
  • October 20 - Steam Next Fest: October 2025 concludes.
  • October 20 - Steam Next Fest: October 2025 Wrap-Up (featuring games with the most played demos of the Fest) launches.

*Dates and times are subject to change.

Changes from Previous Next Fests

As of the October edition of 2024, there are a few changes to Next Fest:
  • There is no more official livestream
  • The developer livestream portion of the event has been shifted to an independent tab -- as always, livestreams are optional
  • The organization of the event is revamped from previous genre tabs, so the page may look different from before

Eligibility

Steam Next Fest: October 2025 is open to upcoming games that meet all of the following criteria:

  • Associated with a Steamworks developer account in good standing
  • The store page must be published and public
  • Is not a prologue, chapter 1, or otherwise 'demo' version of existing content available to customers
  • Includes a publicly playable demo by the time the festival beginsÂ 
  • To be released any time after the conclusion of the fest on October 20, 2025

Please Note:
  • Upcoming games may participate with a playable demo in Steam Next Fest, regardless of release date.
  • Titles are only allowed to participate in one Next Fest. You're welcome to select whichever Next Fest works best for your release time, but you will only be able to participate in ONE. The next edition planned is in February 2026.

See your list of eligible games here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/nextfest_oct_2025

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Are the the live zoom FAQ sessions recorded?
A. Yes! You can check out our past live Q&A sessions on YouTube here. These sessions cover a wide variety of topics to help you prepare for getting the most out of Steam Next Fest.

Q. I haven't received an email invitation and have a game I believe fits the eligibility criteria described above. How can I confirm whether my game has been invited and request an invitation if it has not been invited?
A. If you haven't received an email invitation and believe you have a game that fits the eligibility criteria described above, please check:

  • Your list of eligible games here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/nextfest_oct_2025
  • The registration page for your base game by appending your appID here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/nextfest_oct_2025/<appid>
  • Your base game's App Admin page (in the Marketing Tools & Data section) for the opt-in by appending your appID here: https://partner.steamgames.com/apps/landing/<appID>

If your game is not found via the paths above and you believe it meets the eligibility criteria, please use the appeals process available at the bottom of the registration page.

If your game was found via the paths above but you didn't receive an email, please check your Steamworks communication preferences (instructions available in the Steam Next Fest parent documentation page).

Q. I've changed my plans. How do I cancel my registration?
A. Just return to the registration page for your game by inserting your base game's appID at the end of this URL https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/nextfest_oct_2025/<appid> and deselect the checkbox at the very bottom of the page to remove your game from the festival. After opting out, if you have not released your game prior to a future Steam Next Fest, you could participate in that one assuming the rest of the eligibility requirements are met.
