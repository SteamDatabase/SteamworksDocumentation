Steam Subscriber Agreement

You must include the following language on the packaging of any product requiring user to activate the product on Steam. This language must be prominently displayed on a background color that is different from the primary color of the packaging. For example if the packaging is primarily black the language must be displayed on a background color significantly different than black.

Also note that the text below may not be sufficient to be legally compliant depending on your exact use case and territory. You should consult an attorney if you have questions. Valve does not provide legal advice.

You must activate this product via the Internet by registering for a Steam account and accepting the SSA. Please see https://store.steampowered.com/subscriber_agreement/ to view the SSA prior to purchase. If you do not agree with the provisions of the SSA, you should return this game unopened to your retailer in accordance with their return policy.
